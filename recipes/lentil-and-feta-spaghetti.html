Lentil and Feta Spaghetti

A delicious and nutritious vegetarian pasta recipe that will feel like a warm hug on a cold night.

A perfect balance of vegetables, lentils, and feta, with al dente spaghetti that will be a sure-fire hit with even the pickiest of eaters.

Ingredients

  • 500g Spaghetti
  • 200g Greek feta
  • 400g Canned Brown Lentils
  • 400g Canned Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 Large Carrots
  • 1 Large Brown Onion
  • 4 Garlic Cloves

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water
  2. Finely chop the garlic and onion and add to 50mL olive oil in a deep pan over medium to high heat and cook until fragrant and beginning to become translucent.
  3. While the onion and garlic is cooking, finely dice the carrot, then cook for 3-4 minutes until starting to soften
  4. Drain lentils and add to pan, and once hot, add crushed tomatoes, bringing the sauce to a simmer.
  5. Once sauce has started to thcken, crumble the feta and add to the saucepan, and reduce the heat to low, cover, and allow to simmer.
  6. Meanwhile, add the spaghetti to the pot and cook for 7-10 minutes or until al dente, half way through, adding half a cup of pasta water to the sauce and stirring.
  7. Drain spaghetti and serve hot with a garnish of fresh basil and grated parmesan
  8. Bon Appetit!
